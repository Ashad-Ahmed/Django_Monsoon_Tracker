Monsoon in India!


Monsoon or rainy season, lasting from June to September. The season is dominated by the humid southwest summer monsoon, which slowly sweeps across the country beginning in late May or early June.

Monsoon rains begin to recede from North India at the beginning of October. South India typically receives more rainfall.

This Project demonstrats the use of Django traditional Databases (Sqlite3 in our case) and Other Data Sources (An Excel File in our case) to track average rainfall in India categorized into four regions North India, East India, West India and South India.

We use Sqlite Database to store Historicat Data and an Excel File to keep live data (Current year). Try using Google Sheets, SharePoint or other list tools can be used as well.
